Cal Poly Prepares for 2023 Season

Cal Poly football held their first spring scrimmage of the year on April 22nd. It was their first instance of "Live Play" since the fall, and the mustangs are looking to come out of the gates strong after a dissapointing 2 and 9 record last season. In the offseason, the mustangs moved on from head coach Beau Baldwin and promoted offensive line coach Paul Wulff to be his replacement. Wulff has coached at the collegiate level for 28 years, with 12 of those coming as a head coach. He has hopes of rebuilding Cal Poly's program that has struggled the past couple years, and bring them back to glory.

Cal Poly Breaks The Ground on New John Madden Football Center

On April 29th, Cal Poly officially broke the ground for the New John Madden Football Center. During the ceremony, Cal Poly President Jeffrey Armstrong announched that the 30 million dollar facility is set to be completetd by fall of 2024. The facility will include a locker room, hall of champions, strength and conditioning facilities, coach's offices, and more. The building itself is named after infamous Cal Poly alumni John Madden, who sadly passed away in December of 2021. The new facility is being built adjacent to Mustang Memorial Plaza, a tribute to the 16 Cal Poly football players and team manager who tragically passed away in a 1960 plane crash. The plaza was very special to Madden because many of his friends and temmates lost their lives in the crash, and it is only fitting that a facility in his name is being built right next to a place so special to him.
